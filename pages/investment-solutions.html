Investment Solutions

Divit Finance offers a comprehensive suite of alternative investment strategies designed to deliver attractive risk-adjusted returns, portfolio diversification, and downside protection for sophisticated investors.

Private Credit

Our direct lending platform provides senior secured loans, unitranche financing, and mezzanine debt to middle-market companies. We focus on businesses with stable cash flows, strong management teams, and clear paths to value creation. Target returns of 10-14% with downside protection through senior security positions and comprehensive covenants.

Commercial Real Estate

We invest in value-add and opportunistic commercial real estate across office, multifamily, industrial, and specialty property types. Our hands-on approach includes property repositioning, operational improvements, and strategic leasing to enhance property value. Focus on markets with strong demographic trends and supply-demand imbalances.

Learn More →

Infrastructure

Our infrastructure platform invests in essential assets including renewable energy, digital infrastructure (data centers, cell towers), transportation, and utilities. These investments provide stable, inflation-protected cash flows backed by long-term contracts with creditworthy counterparties. Focus on assets benefiting from secular growth trends and regulatory tailwinds.
